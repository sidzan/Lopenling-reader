What is Daf Yomi? What is Daf Yomi:

Daf Yomi (Hebrew: ימוי ףד, Daf Yomi, "page of the day") is a daily regimen of learning Talmud. All 2,711 pages of the Babylonian Talmud are covered in sequence, in a cycle of seven and a half years. The first cycle of Daf Yomi began on Rosh Hashanah in 1923 across Europe, America, and Israel with the first page of the first tractate of the Talmud, Berakhot. The 14th Daf Yomi cycle starts in January 2020. Daf Yomi (Hebrew: ימוי ףד, Daf Yomi, "page of the day") is a daily regimen of learning Talmud.
