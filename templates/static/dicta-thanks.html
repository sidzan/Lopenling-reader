Sefaria and Dicta אודות ספריא

Sefaria includes algorithms and services provided by Dicta.

Dicta applies cutting edge machine learning and natural language processing tools to the analysis of Hebrew texts. Their objective is to remove the drudgery from the study of classical and modern Hebrew texts, in order to allow researchers to focus on the deeper questions.

Dicta is a non-profit organization that provides its products at no charge for the benefit of the public.

On Sefaria:

  • Tanakh results for Hebrew searches come courtesy of Dicta.
  • Links to parallel texts, and matching of headwords to base texts rely on Dicta algorithms.
  • Much else besides benefits from our ongoing conversations with the Dicta team.

See many other tools for analysis of Hebrew texts on the Dicta Website.
