Director (Israel)

Sefaria is looking for an entrepreneurial and outgoing professional who has a passion for technology, Jewish texts, and education to lead its expansion in the Israeli market. The Director (Israel) is a multi-faceted position with strategy, research, business and partnership development, project management, communications and marketing responsibilities. This person should be fully bilingual in Hebrew and English.

Responsibilities include:

Audience Development

  • Exploring opportunities for (and barriers to) Sefaria’s expanded usage in Israel
  • Overseeing and executing audience development and marketing strategies in Israel
  • Identifying individuals and groups primed for early adoption
  • Developing and enhancing strategic partnerships with Israeli institutions, brands and educators

Outreach and Education

  • Exploring educational technology landscape, identifying relevant partner organizations, publishers, and projects
  • Overseeing educational partnerships and working closely with the Education Coordinator (Israel)

Management

  • Oversight of infrastructure for Israel-based staff, being the primary point person on the ground for the operation in Israel
  • Managing relationships with partners and service providers
  • Communicating with US-based staff on all operational developments
  • Working closely with the US-based staff to write fundraising proposals and preparing regular reports on audience development and outreach and education efforts for funders
  • Meeting with prospective and existing funders

Desired Skills and Experience:

  • Exceptional interpersonal skills
  • Native Hebrew and English speaker, fully bilingual
  • Marketing/communications experience
  • Comfort working in a dynamic, start-up environment
  • University degree
  • At least 10 years of relevant work experience, including at least three-plus years of management experience
  • Documented success managing complex projects
  • Excellent writing and editing skills
  • Strong communication, judgment and decision-making skills
  • Proactive and self-driven worker
  • Ability to troubleshoot and resolve problems
  • Knowledge of the education landscape across Israel (from elementary schools to higher learning institutions) a plus

Travel: Occasional travel and non-traditional hours required

Supervision: Reports to the Chief Marketing and Engagement Officer (based in the US)

Location: Jerusalem

Salary commensurate with experience

To apply: Please send to jobs@sefaria.org your resume and a brief cover letter (cover letter must be in English!) answering the following question: Where do you see Sefaria in Israel in five years? Bilingual resumes will receive priority. Please submit your resume and cover letter as attached documents or pdfs.

About Sefaria: Sefaria is a non-profit organization dedicated to building the future of Jewish learning in an open and participatory way. We are assembling a free living library of Jewish texts and their interconnections, in Hebrew and in translation. With these digital texts, we can create new, interactive interfaces for Web, tablet and mobile, allowing more people to engage with the textual treasures of our tradition.

Communications and Marketing Manager

Position Overview

The Communications and Marketing Manager will take an active role in the research, development, implementation and ongoing maintenance of Sefaria’s digital marketing campaigns and initiatives, including web, SEO/SEM, email, social media and display advertising. Reporting to the Chief Marketing and Engagement Officer, the position will also oversee the measuring and reporting of the performance of digital marketing activity.

The ideal candidate will be a collaborative go-getter with a strong creative drive and top-notch project management skills. Strong writing ability and problem-solving skills are a must. Candidates should be fluent in all social media as this role will manage the day-to-day posting as well as the development and implementation of Sefaria’s editorial calendar. This individual will work closely with all teams to develop communications to support strategic growth, including new product introductions, new and existing library content and user engagement.

The role includes generous medical coverage, dental and eye insurance, healthcare and dependent care flexible spending account benefits, retirement benefits, commuter benefits (New York only), generous PTO, short-term and long-term disability insurance, and employee assistance program. Salary is competitive. Sefaria has a presence in New York, Denver, Chicago, St. Louis, Boston, the Bay Area, and Israel. Remote candidates will also be considered.

Qualifications & Required Skills

As this is a new position at Sefaria, the selected candidate will be a founding member of a growing team. We’re looking for an energetic thought partner who is excited to pitch big ideas and build them together. The position requires a candidate who:

  • Is incredibly organized and can manage a variety of projects with a wide range of deadlines. Can communicate effectively and act as a liaison between the internal team and a number of external vendors.
  • Can manage Sefaria’s blog, email calendar and campaigns and implement a social media strategy across all major platforms. Able to manage both planned posts across a long-term editorial calendar as well as short-turn topical posts.
  • Can write emails, blogs, social media posts and other copy for website and marketing materials.
  • Can deliver metrics reports across social and engagement platforms and make recommendations for improvements.
  • Has experience with Salesforce, NationBuilder or a similar CRM.
  • Has knowledge (and love) of Jewish texts.
  • Hebrew language skills a plus!

Education & Work Experience

  • Bachelor's degree required or equivalent practical experience.
  • At least 3 years of previous experience in related positions.
  • Track record of creating and implementing aggressive growth strategies in the for-profit or nonprofit sectors.
  • Previous experience with SEO, digital strategy, online marketing (email, social media, web), content creation, list building, digital advertising, online fundraising.
  • Experience with Google Analytics, Google AdWords and Facebook advertising a plus.

In your cover letter, we’d love to see your creativity on display--please tell us two things you’d change about our current social media, emails or website and how.
