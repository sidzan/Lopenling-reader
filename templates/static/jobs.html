Part-time Communications & Marketing Associate

About Sefaria

Sefaria is a non-profit organization dedicated to creating the future of Torah in an open and participatory way. We are assembling a free, living library of Jewish texts and their interconnections, in Hebrew and in translation.

Position Overview

The Communications and Marketing Associate is a part-time, temporary contract position through the end of 2020 with the potential to move forward into 2021. This position provides key marketing and communications support for the team’s day-to-day operations as well as major projects and initiatives.

Reporting to the Communications and Marketing Manager, the ideal candidate will be organized, detail-oriented, and able to work both independently and as part of a collaborative team. This position will require 20 hours per week. Compensation is hourly and depends on experience, starting at $20 per hour.

Job Responsibilities

  • Maintain Sefaria’s communications calendar, ensuring the information is up to date and content is going out as scheduled (using Trello, Nation Builder, Buffer)
  • Assist in the management of social media accounts, including drafting content, creating original and template-based graphics in Canva, responding/reacting to comments on our social posts, and monitoring our social channels for spam and hate speech.
  • Measure, track and report on key marketing and communications activities (using Google Analytics, Keyhole, SEMRush)
  • Draft copy for emails, Google ads, and other external communications
  • Manage and provide support for our public webinars (including pre-, mid-, and post-webinar support through Zoom, emails, YouTube, and Sheets)
  • Communicate and coordinate with external vendors
  • Provide general administrative support to Communications and Marketing Manager, and complete other tasks as needed

Qualifications & Requirements

  • Minimum of a two-year degree or equivalent professional experience in a related field
  • Ability to write formal and informal communications for multiple audiences
  • Previous experience with digital marketing (email, social media, web), content creation, and graphic design
  • Experience with Google Analytics, Google AdWords, and Facebook advertising a plus, but not required
  • A personal computer and a stable internet connection are needed for essential job functions and must be provided by the candidate
  • Knowledge of Jewish texts a plus
  • Hebrew language skills a plus

To Apply

Please submit a cover letter and resume or CV to jobs@sefaria.org with the subject line Communications and Marketing Associate. Applications will be accepted on a rolling basis until the position is filled.
