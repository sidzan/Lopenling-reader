About Sefaria אודות בספריא

Sefaria is a non-profit organization dedicated to creating the future of Torah in an open and participatory way. We are assembling a free, living library of Jewish texts and their interconnections, in Hebrew and in translation.

    Summer Engineering Internships

    Position Overview

    Sefaria is looking for two individuals — one in the US and one in Israel — with an interest in Torah and technology to join our engineering team for 8 to 10 weeks. Our paid summer program will expose interns to the inner workings of a fast-growing nonprofit startup.

    Senior engineering staff will help define one or two projects for the intern to develop and manage from start to finish. Interns can expect full support and coaching from the Sefaria team to help meet project goals and grow as engineers.

    Qualifications & Requirements

    • Currently enrolled in a degree program or 2021 graduate
    • Background in computer science or engineering
    • Interest in Jewish learning
    • Solid written and verbal communication skills
    • Strong judgment and decision-making skills
    • English (professional working proficiency)
    • A working computer and internet connection is required for the role and must be supplied by the candidate

    Technologies at Sefaria

    • JavaScript, React, React-Native, D3, HTML, CSS
    • Python, Django
    • MongoDB, Redis, ElasticSearch
    • Kubernetes, Docker, Google Cloud

    Location

    Remote; US or Israel Time Zone

    Commitment & Compensation

    The role will involve 36 hours of work per week for the 8-10 week period. Hourly rate: $15-18/hr.

    Apply (US Position) Apply (Israel Position)

    Communications Associate

    Position Overview

    We’re looking for a highly motivated, creative thinker to join our mission to make Jewish texts accessible and engaging for a diverse and growing audience. The Communications Associate is a full-time, remote position based in the US. This position provides key marketing and communications support for the team’s day-to-day operations as well as major projects and initiatives. Reporting to the Senior Marketing & Communications Manager, the ideal candidate is a strong writer who is equal-parts organized, detail-oriented, and able to work both independently and as part of a collaborative team.

    Job Responsibilities

    • Identify new and innovative opportunities for engaging with our community. Proactively listen and engage in community conversations, identifying audience asks and turning them into potential creative
    • Maintain Sefaria’s communications and launch calendars, ensuring the information is up to date and content is going out as scheduled (using Trello, Nation Builder, MailChimp, Buffer)
    • Assist in the management of social media accounts, including writing content, creating original and template-based graphics in Canva, responding/reacting to comments on our social posts, and monitoring our social channels
    • Write copy for emails, social media, press releases, and other external communications
    • Act as a public-facing representative of Sefaria in live webinars, as well as manage and provide support for webinar operations (including pre-, mid-, and post-webinar support through Zoom, emails, YouTube, and Sheets)
    • Communicate and coordinate with external vendors and contractors
    • Provide general administrative support to the Marketing & Communications Department and complete other tasks as needed

    Qualifications & Requirements

    • Bachelor's degree or equivalent professional experience in a related field
    • 2+ years of experience in marketing, communications, and/or the nonprofit sector
    • Strong writing skills and the ability to write formal and informal communications for multiple audiences
    • Previous experience with content creation and graphic design (canva, adobe creative suite)
    • Some knowledge of Jewish texts
    • Hebrew language fluency a plus

    Location

    The candidate can be based anywhere in the US. Sefaria has a presence in San Francisco, Denver, Chicago, St. Louis, Boston, and New York.

    Compensation & Benefits

    Sefaria offers a compensation package inclusive of a competitive salary, time off, flexible schedule options, and opportunities for professional growth. The salary range for this position is $45,000 - $55,000. Benefits include:

    • Medical, dental and vision insurance
      • Sefaria offers multiple medical plans (Aetna) and at least one plan is 100% employer paid for the employee. Sefaria also contributes a small amount toward premiums for dependents.
    • Generous paid time off, including Jewish holidays.
    • 401k with up to a 4% match after 6 months.
    • Flexible spending accounts for health and dependent care expenses.
    • Short- and long-term disability insurance.
    • Paid parental leave, flexible schedules, and budgets for professional development and work-from-home expenses.

    To Apply

    Apply

    Please submit a cover letter, resume or CV, and two previous writing samples. Priority deadline for applications is May 9th. Applications will be accepted on a rolling basis until the position is filled.
