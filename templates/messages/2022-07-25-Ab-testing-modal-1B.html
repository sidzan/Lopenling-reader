Sefaria Community, קהילת ספריא,

This library was created to make the Jewish canon accessible to all. We rely on the support of our community to grow and maintain the library so everyone can continue learning. We hope you’ll consider donating so that we can continue to grow and nurture this global community of learners by acquiring more texts and improving the platform.
